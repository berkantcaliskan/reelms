Why Reelms

Everything your community needs

Voice & Video Rooms
Crystal-clear voice and video with spatial audio. Hear everyone from their position in the virtual room — just like being there.
Screen Sharing
Share your screen with one click. Support for fullscreen view and remote control — perfect for co-op sessions or remote work.
Activity Detection
Reelms automatically detects what game or app you're running and shares your status — so your friends always know what you're up to.
Spotify Integration
Connect Spotify and let your profile card show what you're listening to in real time. Music is part of the vibe.
Community Spaces
Organize your server with text channels, voice rooms, live spaces, and Vapor Rooms — temporary rooms that vanish when time's up.
Articles & Feed
Write, share, and discover articles within your communities. A built-in feed keeps everyone in the loop.
